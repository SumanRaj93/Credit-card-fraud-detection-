How It Works
01
Data Collection
Real anonymized European card transactions with 284,807 records. Features V1–V28 are PCA components to protect privacy.
02
Undersampling
Dataset has severe imbalance (0.17% fraud). We sample equal numbers of legit & fraud transactions for balanced training.
03
Model Training
Logistic Regression with 80/20 stratified train-test split. Training accuracy: 99.08% with stratified sampling.
04
Prediction
The model outputs a probability score. Transactions above the decision boundary are flagged as potentially fraudulent.
